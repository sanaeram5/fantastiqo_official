What is Fantastiqo
Fantastiqo is a company incorporated in the year 2020 and it is an emerging startup launched in the period of corona pandemic and is becoming more famous and famous due to its uniqueness and creativity.
Motto of the Company
The company is an emerging startup and has started with the motive of providing and creating opportunities for students who are either doing nothing or are just wasting time by playing games or watching movies or websites by whatever means they are called. His main motto is to provide opportunities for students to learn and explore many new things in the area of Social Media Marketing and Graphic Designing. It helps boosting people's interview skills , communication skills and motivates them to come up with better ideas of their own and be a better version of themselves.
Journey of Fantastiqo
Fantastiqo emerged on 9th July , 2020 as an emerging startup and launched its first internship batch on 10th July with 17 interns under it . It started it's area of working with Social Media Marketing and Graphic Designing and then it expertised interns in these respective fields to make them professionals. Fantastiqo got amazing members in the form of interns who worked so hard in their internship and finally, the first internship batch was completed by 15th august. Now, within 2 months Fantastiqo has increased it's area of working an now it's a team of around 50 members.
